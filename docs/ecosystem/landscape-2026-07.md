# World 2.0 Ecosystem and Competitive Landscape

**Research snapshot:** 2026-07-15
**State:** first broad scan; requires continuous verification  
**Decision rule:** similarity does not equal duplication; every comparison is mapped against the actual verified nucleus.

## Executive map

World 2.0 sits at the intersection of six established fields that are usually separated:

```text
wireless/environmental sensing
+ deterministic signal interpretation
+ embodied procedural animation
+ AR/spatial computing
+ persistent or relational companions
+ interactive art / cultural meaning
```

The nearest entities usually dominate only one to three of these layers. The strongest strategic position is therefore not “nobody has done anything similar.” It is:

> Existing companies validate individual layers; World 2.0 must prove a governed causal chain connecting them in a distinctive product and evidence architecture.

## Direct-competitor conclusion

No scanned product was found to publicly demonstrate the exact current chain:

```text
connected-link WLAN telemetry
→ explicit provenance and freshness
→ deterministic state derivation
→ procedural rigged-creature behavior
→ replayable evidence bundle
```

This is **not yet a novelty claim**. It is a scoped observation from the current scan and must be tested against patents, papers, art archives, and product disclosures before public use.

### Commercial-sensing lesson

The market already pays for “radio signals as sensors.” Our commercial distinction cannot be merely **using Wi-Fi**. It must be one or more of:

- embodied real-time interpretation;
- explicit provenance and replay;
- artistic/relational creature design;
- multi-sensor portability;
- developer-facing protocol and tooling;
- unusual experiential or educational application.

### Novelty discipline

These works mean WORLD 2.0 must never claim that it invented:

- sensing people or environments through Wi-Fi/RF;
- mapping CSI to activity or pose;
- using RSSI or channel variation as an environmental signal;
- contactless motion or respiration sensing;
- procedural animation driven by sensor data in general.

Potentially distinctive territory must be documented at the **specific architecture, interface, artistic method, evidence model, or product-mechanic level**.
