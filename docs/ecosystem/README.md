This directory tracks projects, companies, research groups, standards bodies, open-source tools, communities, institutions, venues, partner programs, grants, and adjacent markets relevant to World 2.0.

It is not a decorative link collection. Every entry must answer:

```text
what is it
→ how close is it to our thesis
→ competitor, partner, precedent, tool, venue, or noise
→ what can be learned, reused, contacted, or avoided
→ what evidence supports that classification
```

## Canonical project thesis used for matching

World 2.0 currently means:

```text
real environmental/network observation
→ explicit provenance
→ deterministic feature derivation
→ governed behavior state
→ visible embodiment in a persistent or portable creature
→ reproducible evidence
```

The present verified nucleus uses connected-WLAN telemetry, not Wi-Fi CSI or room-wide RF perception. Future sensing branches may include CSI, BLE, magnetometer, spatial mapping, vision, acoustics, or other adapters only after separate evidence gates.

## Relationship classes

- `DIRECT_COMPETITOR` — substantially overlaps the end-user product or central technical thesis.
- `ADJACENT_COMPETITOR` — competes for users, funding, platform position, or narrative territory.
- `ENABLER` — platform, SDK, protocol, model, hardware, or open-source component we may use.
- `RESEARCH_PRECEDENT` — prior art or scientific work that constrains novelty claims and improves design.
- `CREATIVE_PRECEDENT` — artistic or experiential work relevant to embodiment, participation, invisible signals, or living systems.
- `POTENTIAL_PARTNER` — plausible technical, distribution, research, venue, or funding relationship.
- `INSTITUTION` — lab, center, school, residency, incubator, or cultural organization.
- `COMMUNITY` — recurring practitioner network, conference, forum, standards group, or open-source ecosystem.
- `FUNDING_PATH` — grant, accelerator, partner program, award, residency, or sponsorship route.
- `WATCH_ONLY` — relevant but currently too distant, expensive, closed, uncertain, or strategically premature.

## Current strategic conclusions

1. **Niantic Spatial / Peridot Beyond is the nearest visible product precedent** for an embodied AR companion that perceives and responds to the real world. It is the reference competitor for experience design, not proof that our sensing thesis is redundant.
2. **Origin Wireless and its 2026 acquisition by ADT validate ambient RF sensing as a serious commercial category.** Their focus is security, occupancy, and health rather than creative embodiment.
3. **Snap is presently an enabling platform and distribution relationship, not the owner of the project thesis.** Lens Studio, Camera Kit, and Spectacles remain valuable deployment surfaces.
4. **Espressif `esp-csi` is the most practical first hardware research branch** once the current connected-WLAN causal proof is frozen.
5. **Convai, Inworld, and NVIDIA ACE cover character intelligence and voice layers.** They should not be inserted into the deterministic fast sensing loop.
6. **World Labs and Niantic Spatial define the larger spatial-intelligence narrative.** Our differentiator must be a creature governed by measurable, provenance-preserving environmental change rather than generated 3D alone.
7. **Ars Electronica Futurelab and ZKM Hertz-Lab are unusually close institutional fits** because they combine interactive art, sensing, AR/VR, and electromagnetic-field research.
8. **Epic MegaGrants is the most time-sensitive currently open funding route identified in this scan.** The 2026 Cycle 2 submission window is listed as June 29–September 4, 2026. Engine-fit must be decided honestly before applying.

## Evidence rules

- Prefer official product, laboratory, standards, program, or repository pages.
- Use papers for technical capability claims and independent reporting for acquisitions or market events.
- Do not infer partnership availability from technical similarity.
- Do not call a company a competitor merely because it uses AI, AR, Wi-Fi, or a dragon.
- Record stale or uncertain entries as `WATCH_ONLY` with `verification_required: true`.
- Funding eligibility, sanctions, residency restrictions, deadlines, and corporate requirements must be rechecked at application time.

## Maintenance cadence

A full refresh should occur monthly while the project is pre-revenue and before every:

- grant application;
- investor or partner briefing;
- public novelty claim;
- architecture expansion into CSI, spatial AI, or persistent companions;
- major product-positioning decision.

The scan is deliberately broad, but admission to the critical path remains binary: **Y with evidence and a gate, or N with a written reason.**
